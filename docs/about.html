🌿 About us! 🌿

Making environmental activism is fun and easy

Environeers is a website focusing on increasing everyday actions that help the environment in big ways.
Users of Environeers will become pioneers of environmentalism — hence the name.
By becoming an Environeer, you tell us that you're committed to keeping our earth healthy for future generations.


Environeers is a project that was coded with a team of four people during UT Freetail Hackers' Learnathon 2021.
