Water facts
Did you know?

Less than 1% of the water supplies found on Earth are usable by people. 97% of water is found in the oceans and 2% exists as frozen water in glaciers, icebergs, permafrost, etc.

Tree Facts
Did you know?

Nature facts
Did you know?
